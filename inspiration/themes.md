☀️ Light Mode (Important — don’t just invert colors)

🌤️ Skyforge Light (Recommended Light Theme)
	•	Base: Warm off-white (#F5F7FA, not pure white)
	•	Accent Options:
	•	Blue (primary)
	•	Green (secondary)
	•	Amber (highlight)
	•	Card Style:
	•	Soft shadows instead of neon borders
	•	Slight pixel texture (to keep your theme identity)
	•	Muted gradients (not flat white)
	•	UI Behavior:
	•	No glow effects → replace with elevation (shadows)
	•	Progress bars = solid fills, not neon
	•	Icons = slightly desaturated
	•	Why this works:
	•	Keeps Minecraft-anime identity
	•	Doesn’t feel like a generic SaaS dashboard
	•	Reduces eye strain for daytime use
	•	Tagline: “Forge your skills in daylight.”
